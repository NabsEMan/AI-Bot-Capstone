# Atkinson's Bullion AI Customer Support Bot

> **Capstone Project**: AI Leadership Institute AI Bot Builder Certification  
> **Business Case**: Precious metals dealer needing ethical, educational customer support  
> **Implementation**: ChatGPT Builder (GPT-4.1-mini) + Five Ps Framework

## üéØ Project Overview

**Business Problem**: High demand + supply constraints + regulatory complexity = 
need for 24/7 educational support without providing investment advice.

**Solution**: AI bot that educates about precious metals, guides through processes, 
and maintains strict ethical boundaries.

**Key Innovation**: Strong guardrails prevent investment advice, price forecasting, 
and geopolitical commentary while remaining genuinely helpful.
